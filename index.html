The Depths Within

Don't let your torch go out, when you get hit your torch will go down one level. You have 3 lives.

'k' is sword slash 'j' is run 'wasd' is movement

'R' is reset button. Resets current level. Walk into rocks direction to move it, it can only move once.

If screen is black 'R' resets the game. You can still play if the game is black, but it is difficult.
